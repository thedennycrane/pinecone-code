15 However, the parol evidence rule only applies when it is proved that the

ceases to have an independent existence: Michael Vaz Lorrain v Singapore Rifle

99 Here, the only cause of action Kelington pursues against Mr Gan is breach

RM1,453,565.22 for breaching the LOA; Mr Ganâ€™s case is that he is only liable
for RM24,000. A consent judgment was previously entered for RM24,000 in HC/ORC
