## A S Nordlandsbanken and Another v Robin Hoddle Nederkoorn  
[2001] SGHC 168

Case Number | : | Suit 2040/1994  
---|---|---  
Decision Date | : | 04 July 2001  
Tribunal/Court | : | High Court  
Coram | : |  G P Selvam J   
Counsel Name(s) | : |  Michael Hwang, SC with Christine M Chan (Allen & Gledhill) for the plaintiffs; Thomas Tan (Haridass Ho & Partners) for the defendant   
Parties | : |  A S Nordlandsbanken; Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken London Branch — Robin Hoddle Nederkoorn
